Paul Walker

Paul Walker was an American actor, philanthropist, and car enthusiast best known for his role as Brian O'Conner in the Fast & Furious franchise. Born on September 12, 1973, Walker began his career as a child actor before rising to international fame in the early 2000s.


Beyond the screen, Paul was deeply committed to charitable work. He founded the nonprofit organization Reach Out Worldwide (ROWW), which provides disaster relief in areas struck by natural disasters. His compassion and drive to help others made a lasting impact far beyond Hollywood.
